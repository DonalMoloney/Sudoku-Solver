Sudoku Rules / GAME CONTROLS:
1-9, Keypad 1-9 - Enter the respective number.
DELETE - Clear the board.
SPACE - Solve the board automatically.
ENTER - Place the selected number.
If it's the correct spot, it will be a success; otherwise, you'll get a strike.
If the board is complete, the game will end.

RULES:
1. Fill each row, column, and 3x3 box with the numbers from 1 to 9 without repetition.
2. Use the keyboard numbers or keypad numbers to place a number.
3. If you place a wrong number, you get a strike.
4. The game ends once the board is complete.
